Welcome to Your Student Performance Tool

Hello! This dashboard guides you on how to use your tool and provides useful educational resources for students.

How to Use

  • Press T on the side bar to navigate to the Tracking Page
  • press + for adding groups/subjects/marks.
  • Enter previous scores in the input field.
  • Click "Predict" to see the next expected score.
  • Download the trained model if needed for offline predictions.
Tip: Make sure your previous scores are numbers separated correctly.

Educational Resources & Tools

  • ChatGPT – Ask questions, solve doubts, get explanations in any subject. Use the Plan A trick to break problems into steps for easier solutions.
  • Learn CBSE – Free study material, NCERT solutions, sample papers, and notes for all CBSE subjects.
  • Desmos – Interactive graphing calculator for maths. Plot functions, explore sequences, and visualize equations easily.
  • Byju’s – Video lessons, quizzes, and study guides for CBSE, JEE, NEET, and more.
  • W3Schools Python – Step-by-step Python tutorials, examples, and exercises to learn coding from scratch.
  • Grammarly – Check grammar, punctuation, and improve your English writing for assignments and projects.
Tip: Combine these resources with your performance tool to create a complete study workflow. For example, use ChatGPT for doubts, Desmos for math visualization, and Grammarly for writing clarity.
